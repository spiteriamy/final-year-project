A conversational grammar tutor for Latin

Talk2MeLatin is a chatbot that answers natural-language questions about the morphology of Latin words — their part of speech, tense, mood, case, and more.

help

What you'll do

Chat with the bot, asking it questions about Latin words you're curious about. There's no script — explore freely.

schedule

How long it takes

Around 10–15 minutes. You'll be asked to fill out a short feedback form afterwards.

Example questions you could ask

  • "What part of speech is amat?"
  • "What case is puellam?"
  • "What tense is vocabat?"
